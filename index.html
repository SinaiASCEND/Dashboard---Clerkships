The Views

Three ways into the same data

Each view answers a different question. Start wide with the cohort, narrow to a single clerkship, or step outside to the national picture — the survey items and benchmark coding stay consistent across all three.

Cohort overview

All Clerkships

Every clerkship on one screen: the eight clerkships as rows, all ten survey items as columns, grouped under their four LCME elements. Each cell shows the year-to-date rate and its change from last year, color-coded against the benchmark.

Answers: Across every clerkship, where are the strengths and the soft spots right now? Any row opens that clerkship's module detail.

  • 8 clerkships × 10 items
  • YTD rate + year-over-year
  • Click a row to drill in
Single clerkship

Monitoring Matrix

Pick one clerkship and examine it three ways — rolled up by LCME element, broken out by teaching site, and tracked by module across the four modules of the year. Every rate is color-coded against an adjustable benchmark you set.

Answers: How is this clerkship performing, and where is the variation — between sites, or across the year?

  • By element
  • By site
  • By module (1–4)
  • Adjustable benchmark
National comparison

National Benchmark

The cohort's AAMC Graduation Questionnaire results set beside national figures, across four measures: overall quality of the educational experience, being observed taking a history, being observed performing an exam, and mid-clerkship feedback. Each has a year-filterable bar graph.

Answers: How do our students' experiences compare with their peers nationally, and how is that trending?

  • AAMC GQ data
  • 4 measures
  • Filter by year

How the data is organized

Four elements, eight clerkships

Every survey item maps to one of four LCME elements. The same ten items run through all of the views, so a number means the same thing wherever you see it.

9.7 Formative Assessment & Feedback Received feedback · Feedback helpful · CPE form completed
9.4 Direct Observation Observed taking a history · Observed performing an exam
9.3 Clinical Supervision Adequately supervised · Supervisor available

About the data. The 2025–26 year-to-date figure is the full-year average across all four modules; 2024–25 is the referent for year-over-year comparison.

The two site-monitored elements (9.3 and 3.5) are pooled across teaching sites weighted by respondent count, using the same function the rest of the dashboard uses — nothing is invented at the clerkship level.
